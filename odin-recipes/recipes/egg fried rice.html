How to Make Delicious Egg Fried Rice

Ingredients Needed:

  • Eggs
  • Onion (chopped)
  • Cooked rice
  • Soy sauce and salt

Steps:

  1. Scramble the eggs in a hot pan and set aside.
  2. Saut√© the onions until they soften.
  3. Add the cooked rice, soy sauce, and salt, and stir well.
  4. Mix in the scrambled eggs and cook for another 1-2 minutes.
