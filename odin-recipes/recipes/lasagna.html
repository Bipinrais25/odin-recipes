Lasagna

Delicious Lasagna, very italian...

Ingredients Needed:

  • Cheese (mozzarella and parmesan)
  • Eggs
  • Meat (ground beef or sausage)
  • Veggies (e.g., bell peppers, mushrooms)
  • Cream or milk
  • Lasagna sheets

Steps

  1. Cook the meat with veggies, add tomato sauce, and simmer.
  2. Make white sauce by whisking cream with butter and flour until thick.
  3. Mix cheese and eggs in a bowl.
  4. Layer lasagna sheets, meat sauce, white sauce, and cheese mixture in a baking dish.
  5. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 30-40 minutes, removing foil for the last 10 minutes.
