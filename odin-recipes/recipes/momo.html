Momo

Nepalese Style Dumplings

Ingredients Needed:

  • Meat (minced)
  • Onion (finely chopped)
  • Garlic and ginger (minced)
  • Dough (for wrapping)
  • Achar (spicy dipping sauce)

Steps to Make Momo

  1. Mix the meat, onion, garlic, and ginger together for the filling.
  2. Wrap the filling in small pieces of dough to form dumplings.
  3. Steam the dumplings for about 10-15 minutes. Serve with achar.
